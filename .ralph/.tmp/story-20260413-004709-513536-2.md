### S3: Execute one bounded hardening iteration
Status: in_progress
Depends on: S2

Description:
Support a single defensive iteration that applies one small, high-confidence security improvement or mitigation. The iteration must remain scoped, reversible in intent, and linked back to the prioritized decision.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] The selected action is constrained to a single bounded change or mitigation for the current iteration.
- [ ] The loop defines how to verify the action completed successfully without broadening scope.
- [ ] Unsafe or ambiguous actions are blocked and recorded instead of executed.
- [ ] The iteration output includes enough detail for a human to understand what changed or what was deliberately skipped.
