### S2: Prioritize defensive next action
Status: in_progress
Depends on: S1

Description:
Create a simple prioritization model that converts raw findings or observations into one bounded defensive action per iteration. The model should favor actions that reduce real exposure quickly and avoid noisy or low-impact work.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] The system can rank candidate defensive actions using explicit factors such as severity, exploitability, exposure, confidence, and implementation safety.
- [ ] The loop selects exactly one primary action for the iteration and records why alternatives were not chosen.
- [ ] Low-confidence or unverifiable findings are marked for follow-up instead of being treated as confirmed risk.
- [ ] The prioritization output is human-reviewable and suitable for demo presentation.
