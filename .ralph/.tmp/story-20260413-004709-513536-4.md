### S5: Close the loop with next-step guidance
Status: in_progress
Depends on: S4

Description:
Provide a compact end-of-iteration summary that tells the operator what to do next. The summary should make the loop feel complete for a demo while still setting up the next pass in a disciplined way.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] The iteration ends with a concise summary of what risk was reduced, what evidence supports that claim, and what remains.
- [ ] The loop recommends the next best defensive action or states clearly why it cannot continue safely.
- [ ] The summary is short enough for a buildathon demo and precise enough for technical review.
- [ ] The final output reinforces that the product is a defensive iteration loop, not just a scanner or report generator.
