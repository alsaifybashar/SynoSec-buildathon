### S4: Record evidence and residual risk
Status: in_progress
Depends on: S3

Description:
After each iteration, store the evidence needed to justify the action and determine what remains unresolved. This should make the next iteration faster and prevent repeated work or unsupported claims.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] The loop records source findings, the chosen action, verification evidence, and final outcome for the iteration.
- [ ] Residual risks and deferred items are captured in a structured, reviewable format.
- [ ] The record distinguishes between fixed issues, mitigated issues, unverified issues, and intentionally skipped issues.
- [ ] A future iteration can start from the recorded output without reconstructing prior context manually.
